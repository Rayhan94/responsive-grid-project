About

Rayhan Miah grew up in London he is passionate about Art and Software. A self taught artist from London from as little as of 4 years old he can remember there has been a fascination in visual arts after seeing his teacher draw something with vibrant colours. He has a style of classical/traditional with a unique mixture of modern contemporary art. His style consists of realism, impressionism and psychedelics from sketches, drawings and paintings. His artistry is characterized by a concern with depicting the visual impression of the moment, especially in terms of the shifting effect of light and color a literary or artistic style that seeks to capture a feeling or experience rather than to achieve accurate depiction.
